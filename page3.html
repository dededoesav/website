Plaster Cast Theatre

I was first brought onto to collaborate with Plaster Cast on their verbatim show Sound Cistem at Niamos for the event Stream Econ.
Since then, the show with visuals has toured across the UK, performing at the Brighton fringe, Brainchild Festival and Pleasance in London.
I am currently in R&D for Delicious Fruits, a new verbatim piece that will be performed alongside Sound Cistem with Pleasance Theatre at the Edinburgh Fringe throughout August.
